# 🧴 Oil up Jeffrey

**Oil up Jeffrey** is a fast-paced reaction game where timing, focus, and precision decide your score.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

Your goal is simple:

* Hold down your mouse (or touch on mobile)
* Move the baby oil bottle over Jeffrey to "oil him up"
* Build up your score by completing smooth oiling strokes

But there’s a catch:

👀 **Jeffrey will randomly look at you**

* If you move while he’s looking → **Game Over**
* If you lose, Jeffrey will oil you up
* It doesn’t matter if you're clicking or not — any movement counts

---

## ⚡ Features

* 🖱️ Mouse + 📱 Touch support (mobile & tablet)
* 🔊 Sound effects (brush, alert, game over, highscore)
* 💧 Particle effects (oil droplets)
* 🎉 Confetti effect on new highscore
* 📊 Local highscore saving (via browser storage)
* 📤 Share your highscore directly to Twitter/X
* 🔁 Reset highscore button
* 💥 Screen shake on game over

---

## 🧠 Mechanics

* Score increases by completing full baby oil bottle strokes (up/down motion)
* Randomized "look" system keeps gameplay unpredictable
* Reaction window gives you a short time to stop moving
